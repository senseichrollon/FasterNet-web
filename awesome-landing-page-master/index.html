Features

Completely Automatic

Fasternet detects the best local DNS nameservers to use, and then automatically applies the configuration to your Windows PC - you just have to click Start.

Zero Storage

Fasternet runs entirely from your PC's temporary storage and clears itself on exit. Download the exe, run it, and forget about it.
